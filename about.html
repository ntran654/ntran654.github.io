Hello! My name is Nhung Tran. If you're curious about the pronunciation of my name, it is the sound of a car speeding down a street (nyoom)!
I enjoy browsing the internet, digital media, traditional art, and I'm interested in your dog. Currently, I serve as one of the eleven Key Club International Trustees from the CNH KIWIN'S District. Every day, I am able to challenge my creativity with the work presented to me by my position, and by my surroundings. I have professional experience in graphic design, video production, and I want to improve on my skills in web design. I want to study Communications or Computer Science in the future, with my ultimate goal being employment at Google!
